My name is Sanjay. Most people who know me would say that I am passionate about programming, robotics, and STEM education. I am also passionate about history and enjoy photography.

I have been programming small LEGO robots for almost a decade now. Within five years, I became an expert in the field. I was invited by The LEGO Group to become one of the youngest ever MINDSTORMS Community Partners (MCP), a small group of experts invited to collaborate with LEGO on projects. I began my own website, EV3Lessons in 2014 and started teaching other children how to program LEGO robots. Today, I have a million users across all my websites and community groups. I have been invited by The LEGO Group and LEGO Education to participate in numerous events and conferences around the world to inspire others and share my knowledge. This expertise led to becoming a technical editor for a LEGO robotics book. Most recently, I was invited to the First 5, a group of 5 community members to help design and test a brand new LEGO robotics platform called SPIKE Prime. This product was launched in January 2020. I now also run a website called primelessons.org which hosts programming lessons for SPIKE Prime.

2020 marks my tenth year in FIRST robotics. My team won the highest award in the program - World Championâ€™s in 2018. From LEGO robots, I became interested in large metal robots in FIRST Robotics Competition. In 2018, I joined an FRC team and became the Lead Programmer within months. I founded my own team in 2019 in Sharon, PA and I am the Team Captain and the Lead Programmer. FIRST Robotics is an amazing program because it teaches programming, engineering, design and lifeskills. My team, FRC 8027, qulified for the World Championships in 2020, but it was cancelled due to COVID-19. I was also a Dean List semi-finalist list year through this program.

When I am not with my robotics team, I am most likely conducting a science experiment. I have been participating in science fairs since eight grade. I am both a Broadcom MASTERS finalist and an INTEL ISEF finalist. These are the premiere international science fairs for students. All my projects are related to computer science and engineering.

For fun, I enjoy traveling and taking photographs. Majority of the photographs on this website were taken by me. I love experiencing the sights and sounds of a new country and studying its history. While I have been involved in robotics and science fairs, I actually also enjoy history. I founded the History Club at my high school. You can read my blog post on the subject.

I hope you will enjoy the posts on this page.
